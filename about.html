Our Platform

Founded in 2003 in Beirut, ODYSSEY.LB has grown from a small Lebanese startup to one of the biggest leading digital travel companies. ODYSSEY.LB’s mission is to make it easier for everyone to experience the life in Lebanon. We know travel can be hard, but we also know that it’s worth it, every time. And because we believe travel is a force for good, we take our roles seriously. We’re here to build great products, and facilitate connections between travelers and our partners that truly bring good into Lebanon.

We build connections. We leverage our platform and technology capabilities across an extensive portfolio of businesses and brands to orchestrate the movement of people and the delivery of travel experiences on both a local and global basis. We help our travelers and our partners find the right pathways to enjoy the experience in Lebanon.

We are dedicated to helping travelers make the most of their visit to this beautiful country by offering a wide range of experiences to suit every interest and budget. From hiking and skiing to exploring ancient ruins and vibrant cities, our website makes it easy to browse and book your adventure. We also provide useful information on visa requirements, local customs, and safety tips to ensure a hassle-free trip. Choose Odyssey.lb for an unforgettable travel experience in Lebanon.
